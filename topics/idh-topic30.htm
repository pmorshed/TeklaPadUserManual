Tab2: C# Console

This tab is a tiny compiler which allows you to create console application or plug-in.

How?! In brief:

 

Choose your .Net Framework from available choices. Then the most common assemblies will be added automatically. You can also add/remove assemblies according to your demand.

Write your code.

Good news: An intellisense (code-completion) feature is also available to help you.

Run your program (or press F5)

Get the result.

Note: TeklaPad will be minimized to tray while your compiled program is running. It will be restored automatically after terminating of your program.

 

 

 

Caution

You should be careful about compatibility and dependencies while working (add/remove/...) with assemblies.
